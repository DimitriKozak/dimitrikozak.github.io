THE TANGANYIKA ANGLING CHALLENGE (TAC)

Tanganyika Angling Challenge is a 6 day fishing competition held in Nsumbu and Nsumbu National Park in February / March annually .It is known as one of the he most prestigious fishing competition in Zambia. Members limited to 3 per boat and boats are available for hire.
It is focused of a catch and release basis and focuses highly on conservation through Nsumbu Tanganyika Conservation Project, a Frankfurt Zoological Society programme. Any extra funds are put towards conservation of Nsumbu National Park and Lake Tanganyika.

Competition Categories

The Competition Consist of 3 Categories over the 6 day

Hyperlight

(2lb/1kg line) 1 Day

Ultralight

(4lb/2kg line) 2 Days

Nationals

(12/6kglb) 3 Days

COMMON SPECIES CAUGHT

Nile Perch, Lake Salmon , Nkupi/yellowbelly (, Tigerfish, Bream, Lake Trout ,Vundoo, Frontosa, Mbilli and a variety of other fish.

The competition has a long history, running since the 1980's and we wish to carry this on for years to come..

The Tanganyika Angling Challenge is a great way to show of your fishing skills and prove to be a competitive angler.

Nkamba Bay Lodge is very proud to be part of the Lake Tanganiyka Challenge and look forward to seeing all the competitors every year. We welcome back seasoned competitors and welcome any new anglers to join this wonderful competition.
